# CartCoroner AI

## Behavioral Revenue Intelligence for Shopify

CartCoroner AI is an AI-powered behavioral intelligence engine that performs autopsies on abandoned Shopify carts to identify hidden checkout friction and autonomously recommend targeted recovery strategies.

Instead of sending generic recovery emails, CartCoroner diagnoses *why* customers abandon purchases by analyzing behavioral signals such as shipping hesitation, pricing anxiety, trust gaps, and decision paralysis.

---

# The Insight

Most cart recovery tools treat abandonment as the problem.

CartCoroner treats abandonment as the symptom.

By identifying the behavioral friction causing conversion failure, merchants can fix systemic revenue leakage instead of guessing with discounts.

- AI-powered behavioral autopsies

- Revenue impact estimation
